A decision-making tool for the age we actually live in.

Three counts. Hidden fists. Reveal.

## Play

Visit: **https://hopefulhumanist-chooser.github.io/pawn-serf-sheep**

## About

Pawn, Serf, Sheep is a two-player hand gesture game built on the same foundation as rock paper scissors — a simple cycle of dominance between three equal forces. Where rock paper scissors resolves a moment, this game resolves a situation.

- **Pawn** beats Serf
- **Serf** beats Sheep
- **Sheep** beats Pawn
- **Nobody** beats the Overlord

The Overlord is not a variable. The Overlord is the climate.

## How to play

1. Tap Begin
2. Player one chooses a gesture in secret
3. Pass the phone to player two
4. Player two chooses a gesture in secret
5. Both players watch the five second countdown together
6. Reveal
